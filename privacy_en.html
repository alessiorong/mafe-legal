2. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data:

This data is entered voluntarily by the user and is not automatically retrieved from real bank accounts.

If you use the "Spending Groups" feature, you may enter the name, email and phone number of other people (participants). Important: you are responsible for obtaining consent from those individuals before entering their data into the app. This data is used exclusively for group balance calculations and is not shared with third parties.

2.4 Receipt Images

If you use the receipt scanning feature, the image is transmitted to third-party AI services (see §5) for data extraction. Images are not permanently stored on our servers after processing.

2.5 Technical and Usage Data

  • Device token for push notifications (stored locally)
  • App preferences (theme, currency) — stored locally on device
  • Anonymous error logs for service maintenance
  • AI categorisation feedback (accepted/modified category)

6. Your Rights

Under EU Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Access (Art. 15): you may request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification (Art. 16): you may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure / Right to be forgotten (Art. 17): you may request deletion of your data directly from the app (Settings → Delete Account) or by email.
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18): you may request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Portability (Art. 20): you may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON) to transfer to another service. Use Settings → Export my data.
  • Objection (Art. 21): you may object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Withdrawal of consent: where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise your rights, write to: support@mafebalance.com. We will respond within 30 days of receipt.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority. In Italy: Garante per la protezione dei dati personali — www.garanteprivacy.it. In your country of residence, the relevant national data protection authority applies.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) for all communications
  • Passwords stored exclusively as hashes (bcrypt)
  • Row Level Security (RLS) on all database tables — each user accesses only their own data
  • Secure JWT authentication with expiry
  • Data access limited to the minimum necessary (data minimisation principle)

Despite these measures, no system is completely invulnerable. In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights, we will notify you promptly as required by Art. 34 GDPR.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Mafe Balance is a mobile application and does not use cookies. Some app preferences (theme, currency) are stored locally on your device via SharedPreferences and are not transmitted to external servers.

9. Minors

The service is intended for persons aged 18 or over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a minor has provided personal data without parental consent, please contact us immediately at support@mafebalance.com and we will arrange for its deletion.

10. Changes to this Policy

The updated version will always be available in the app under Settings → Privacy Policy.
